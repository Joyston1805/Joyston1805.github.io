## The problem

Atticus Bakery targets a 2% buffer for packaging overage on finished product. Anything beyond that is wasted material and margin.

## The approach

- Pulled production and packaging logs into Excel
- Built a rolling view of actual overage vs. the 2% target line
- Isolated the SKUs and shifts contributing most to the gap

## The finding

Actual overage was running **1.9 percentage points** above the buffer — enough to matter at scale, but invisible without a dedicated report.

## The fix

A recurring KPI file tracking yield, scrap, and line performance gave operations leadership a standing view of the metric, instead of a one-time audit.
